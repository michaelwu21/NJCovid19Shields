3D Printing

1) Fill out the Form

FORM LINK
This form helps us keep track of who is
printing and their contact information

2) Download the Parts

Download STLS:
1) covid19_headband_rc3.stl
2) bottom_reinforcement.stl
Files courtesy of Prusa's Face Shield Design

3) Print the Parts

Follow our instructions below↓
for details in print settings
Print as many as you can!

4) Sanitize the Parts

Use 70%+ isopropyl alcohol
for 1+ minute to clean the parts
(This step is a precaution - If you cannot,
make sure you indicate so in the form)

5) Package the Parts

Once you have printed a good amount (20+), package the parts. Any packaging will work!

6) Ship the Parts

SHIPPING INFORMATION
Indicate in the form if you need reimbursement
